Harriet's Life and Legacy

  • 1822: Born Araminta Ross in Dorchester County, Maryland
  • 1844: 1st marriage to John Tubman. The marriage ended a few years later due to John not wanting to join her escape
  • 1849: 1st escape attempt with her brothers, Ben and Henry
  • 1850-1860: The years of The Underground Railroad
  • 1859: Purchased her first home in Auburn, New York
  • 1860-1865: The Civil War. Served as a spy, scout, nurse, and cook for the US Army
  • 1869: Tubman's second marriage to Nelson Davis. Eventually adopted a baby girl named Gertie
  • 1869: Women's movement, became more involved in the women's sufferage movement working alongside Susan B. Anthony
  • 1913: Passed away on March 10, 1913, in Auburn, New York
